My top 3 movies of all time

Athadu

This is a Telugu movie directed by Trivikram and is an action packed film.

3 Idiots

This is a Hindi movie directed by Rajkumar Hirani. It really makes us question the education system and if it is really achieving what is was meant to do in the first place.

Taare Zaameen Par

This is another Hindi movie produced by the famous actor Aamir Khan. A faithful recreation of how Indian parents pressure children into depression.
